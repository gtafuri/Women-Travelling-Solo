Travel Tips

  • Be aware of the culture of your destination!
  • Keep your valuables on you while in transit.
  • Only take what you need and leave the rest locked up.
  • Don't trust people too quickly.
  • Drink responsibly.
  • Make copies of important documents.
  • Keep your friends and family updated.
  • Be wary of public Wi-Fi.
  • Be aware of your surroundings.
  • Monitor credit cards and reports.
  • Protect your home while you're away.
  • Don't wear flashy jewelry.
  • Bring travel locks.
  • Always carry a portable charger.
  • Get travel insurance.
  • Pack a first aid kit.
  • Bring pepper spray.
  • Don't hitch hike.
  • Try not to travel at night.
  • Make sure you know what the taxi cabs look like.
  • Be discreet when map reading.
